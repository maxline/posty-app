# Why I Like Coding

Coding is one of the few crafts where an idea can become something real and
usable in just a few lines. There's a unique satisfaction in taking a vague
concept and turning it into working software.

## Solving puzzles

Every bug and every feature request is a little puzzle. Figuring out *why*
something isn't working, or *how* to build something new, is endlessly
rewarding.

## Constant learning

The tools, languages, and best practices keep evolving, which means there's
always something new to learn. Coding keeps you curious.

## Building things that matter

Whether it's a small script that saves time or a full application people rely
on, coding lets you create things that make a real difference.
